Sandeepan

Graduate Student | Sociology | London School of Economics and Political Science

I am a graduate student in the Department of Sociology at London School of Economics and Political Science(LSE).At present, I am working on my dissertation towards the completion of my degree in MSc Sociology.

I have received training in qualitative research methodology and transcultural studies at LSE and Karl Jaspers Centre for Advanced Transcultural Studies respectively. Sociologically I am influenced by Bourdieu, Burawoy, Srinivas, Dumont and Durkheim.

However , I am inclined towards an interdisciplinary understanding of contemporary society. Apart from an academic necessity I use sociology to make sense of quotidian events occurring around me. Previously I have been associated with Ravenshaw University, Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Central University of Hyderabad and Heidelberg University.
